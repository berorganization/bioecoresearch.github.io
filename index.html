Bioinformatics and Ecoinformatics Research

About

The Bioinformatics and Ecoinformatics Research is an initiative to provide computer applications and standardization to develop and advance data-driven research.

Usage

We provide computer applications under open source licenses and curated datasets for modelling in small or large scale, depending on the needs of the user.

Computer applications

List of computer applications part of the MELM package.

Macro Ecological Large Modeller (MELM)

Download executablesDownload source codeDocumentation

MELM is the main computer application provided by the Bioinformatics and Ecoinformatics Research. It can be used to model complex ecological systems and make predictions using biological and environmental data.

Dataset converter

Download executablesDownload source codeDocumentation

This is a command-line computer application that allows to convert the format of public datasets to a format readable by MELM.

Datasets

These datasets are available as standard choices for research. They are curated and regularly updated. The workflow to produce these same datasets is available at the standard documentation.

Taxonomy

Download latest versionSee all versionsDocumentation

The taxonomy dataset contains entries representing clades and their ascendant relatives. The dataset is curated and generated from the NCBI taxonomy dataset and Tree of Life.

Semantics

Download latest versionSee all versionsDocumentation

The semantics dataset contains entries of terms and their relationship. The scope includes environmental terms and biological terms. The dataset is curated and generated from the ontology datasets found in the OBO Foundry.

Geography

Download latest versionSee all versionsDocumentation

The geography dataset contains multiple matrices for curated geographical information of topography, atmospheric temperature, atmospheric currents, atmospheric precipitation, aquatic temperature, aquatic currents, geological composition.
